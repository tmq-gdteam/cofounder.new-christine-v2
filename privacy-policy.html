The information you provide may include your name and email address, along with any message or details you choose to share. When you visit this website, certain information may be collected automatically through standard web technologies. This may include general technical details such as browser type, device type, and basic usage information about how the website is accessed and navigated. This website may include forms such as an early-access or waitlist form and a contact form. Information submitted through these forms is used to respond to your request, to provide updates you have asked for, and to operate and improve the website and the early-access program.

How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to respond to your requests and messages, to operate and maintain the website, to provide early-access updates and related communications you have requested, to understand and improve how the website is used, and to protect the website and its users.
