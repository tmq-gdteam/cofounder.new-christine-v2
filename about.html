About

Founders building alone deserve an execution partner.

cofounder.new starts from a practical belief: the hardest part of building is not getting advice. It is turning direction into visible, reviewable progress while every important decision still belongs to the founder.

THE BELIEF

Smaller teams need stronger operating systems.

The next generation of companies will be built by founders who use AI not as another noisy chat box, but as an operating layer for strategy, product, growth, operations, and accountability.

The operating loop

From intent to progress.

A simple operating model designed to turn goals into accountable tasks.

Workflow loop
Founder direction → Agent workstreams → Approval queue → Visible progress

Principles

How the system is built.

Momentum over advice

The system is designed to turn goals into next actions, drafts, workstreams, and progress updates.

Control over chaos

Important decisions stay visible, reviewable, and approval-gated.

Workflows over chat

The product should feel less like a blank conversation and more like a founder operations room.

Leverage before hiring

The goal is to give solo builders and small teams the feel of a strategist, operator, growth lead, and product partner before they can hire the full team.
