About

Founders building alone deserve an execution partner.

cofounder.new starts from a practical belief: the hardest part of early company building is not getting advice. It is turning direction into visible, reviewable progress while every important decision still belongs to the founder.

Built around momentum and control.

The product is being shaped for solo and early-stage founders who already know how noisy disjointed AI tools can feel. A cofounder metaphor only works if the work is accountable. That means clear state, clear ownership, and clear permission moments.

The website avoids invented team bios, credentials, testimonials, and metrics. It focuses on the operating model: founder direction goes in, structured work comes out, and sensitive actions stay approval-gated.
