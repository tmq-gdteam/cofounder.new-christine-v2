iCalendly

When AI schedules for customers, the company behind it needs systems too.

iCalendly shows how an AI-powered scheduling product could use cofounder.new to organize onboarding, technical support, customer education, and product feedback loops.

Category
AI scheduling automation
Audience
Service businesses, clinics, salons, and sales teams

Challenge

AI scheduling sounds simple to the customer: fewer no-shows, better confirmations, and less manual chasing.

But behind the scenes, voice calls, SMS messages, email automation, reminder logic, customer preferences, and setup questions can create a heavy operational load. Nontechnical business owners need onboarding that feels simple, even when the product is technically complex.

Opportunity

iCalendly is a strong example of “AI scaling AI.” The product uses AI to help customers automate scheduling, while cofounder.new could help the business itself organize support, onboarding, education, and product iteration.

Case study takeaway

AI scheduling tools face intense onboarding friction from nontechnical users. cofounder.new is designed to help turn messy support channels into structured educational campaigns and clear onboarding sequences, allowing founders to focus on product instead of repetitive support.
