Challenge

A discount aggregator can look simple from the outside. Behind the scenes, it requires constant operational work: finding tools, checking codes, updating pages, contacting partners, managing affiliate links, and creating search-friendly content.

If the founder has to do all of that manually, growth is capped by available hours.

Opportunity

HalfOff.ai is a strong conceptual example of a business where cofounder.new could turn repetitive media and affiliate operations into structured agent-led workflows.

Instead of manually managing every deal, the business could use agents to find new AI tools, draft partner outreach, prepare deal pages, check expired codes, and summarize affiliate performance.

AI Agent Team Used
